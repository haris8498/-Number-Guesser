How to Play

Welcome to Number Guesser - the ultimate number guessing challenge!

  • The computer has selected a random secret number within the range shown.
  • Your goal is to guess the number in as few attempts as possible.
  • After each guess, you'll get feedback:
    • Too Low - Your guess is below the secret number
    • Too High - Your guess is above the secret number
    • Correct! - You guessed the right number!
  • Use the slider to visualize where your guess falls in the range.
  • Try different difficulty levels for more challenge!
  • Each correct guess earns you points based on remaining guesses and difficulty.

Good luck! Can you beat the high score?
